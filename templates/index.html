Poker Table Positions

Button (BTN)
Best position. Acts last post-flop. Maximum information and control.
Small Blind (SB)
Acts first post-flop. Worst position overall despite acting late pre-flop.
Big Blind (BB)
Last to act pre-flop. Gets best pot odds but poor post-flop position.
Late Position
Includes Cutoff (CO) and positions near button. Strong stealing positions with good information.
Middle Position
Middle positions (MP, MP+1). Moderate strength. Balance between early and late positions.
Early Position
Includes Under the Gun (UTG) and UTG+1. First to act pre-flop. Requires strongest hands.

Position affects hand ranges, betting frequencies, and overall strategy
